2 IIR Study Cycle

2.1 Project Start Up

  • Idea is propose and is sent to either Larissa Stanberry or Ross Garberich. IIR project request form is filled out. IIR project intake form is filled out. Study budget form is filled out.

  • Proposal Gets sent to Sub-Committee Member. Gets approved by Sub-committee member. If not approved, review action items, communicate with PI and resubmit if PI desires.

  • IRB Submission. Typically research associates will complete this.

  • IRB Approved. Study Starts.

Data

  • REDCap or Excel created to manage the data. Work with scientific services team (IIR Team) for building of REDCap if needed.

  • Determine source of the data.

  • Collect data and store in either REDCap or Excel, based on the project protocol.

Stats

  • Collected Data is handed off to Biostatisticians.

  • Meet regularly with the Stats team to discuss findings, tables, graphs, figures, etc…

Publication

  • Manuscript/Abstract Writing is done here.

  • Consult with research team about findings and message. Submit to desired journal.

Dissemination

  • Once published, the manuscript/abstract will be collected into our data base for dissemination.

  • Presentations at conferences may also be an opportunity for dissemination of the project.

2.2 Research Project Cycle

Study Intake

  • Each project starts with an idea!

  • Submit your proposal & budget to IIR for initial review

Research Committee

  • Reviews the proposal and decides on approval

IIR & Regulatory

  • IIR team prepares an IRB submission.

  • PI team reviews the draft.

  • MHIF regulatory reviews and submits to Allina IRB

Allina IRB

  • Review the submitted study.

  • Review process takes 4-6 weeks

  • MHIF has no control over it

Data management

  • IIR helps with data pulls from EDW

  • IIR sets up REDCap database and can assist with data abstraction

  • Data abstraction requires strict adherence to protocol

  • Any additional variables, aims or changes, require IRB amendment

Data Analysis

  • Stat team provide statistical support

  • Work with the statistician to analyze data, interpret results, prepare manuscripts

Disseminations

  • IIR Team can assist with abstracts, posters, and manuscripts

  • Your publications appear on our monthly publications list
